# CIVE202_Project3

The Federal Highway Administration (FHWA) has tasked our firm with organizing and visualizing transportation data from the National Household Travel Survey (NHTS) and the Next Generation Simulation (NGSIM) prior to a meeting of the transportation planning group in our firm meeting. We have been provided two different CSV files and two Metadata files that explain the content of the data sets. 
Specifically, the PE has asked to complete the following tasks:
-	A bar chart comparing categories within a specific variable 
-	A histogram showing the distribution of a specific variable 
-	A boxplot showing the distribution of a variable in each category 
-	At least two different time-series plots showing and analyzing driving behavior 

A simulation study using the Intelligent Driver Model (IDM) was performed to visualize and simulate vehicle behavior and trajectories. The acceleration of the follower was calculated using the IDM equations and the results were visualized on a plot.

A timetable documentation of how long the data analysis takes in Python was also created to compare to similar Excel methods.
